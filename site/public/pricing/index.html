Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an entry?

An entry is a single piece of data appended to a ledger. Each append operation counts as one entry, regardless of data size (up to 1MB per entry).

What is external anchoring?

External anchoring publishes your ledger's root hash to a public blockchain (Bitcoin/Ethereum) or transparency log, providing independent proof of your data's existence.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Downgrading may require reducing ledger count.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes, all Pro plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can continue with the free tier if Pro isn't right for you.

What's included in self-hosted?

The self-hosted version includes all core features: append-only storage, cryptographic proofs, and the REST API. External anchoring and webhooks are included but require your own configuration.

Do you offer discounts?

We offer annual billing (2 months free), nonprofit discounts (50% off), and startup programs. Contact us for details.
